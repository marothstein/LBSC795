VoiceOver Navigation of Umd.edu

We were tasked with finding the credit value of the HCIL Masters Capstone Project / Thesis by navigating from http://www.umd.edu with no visual input. We used only the OS/device's native accessibility features to accomplish this. I used a MacBook Pro (OSX Lion) and an iPad (1st Gen) to complete the task. Both devices were equipped with Apple's VoiceOver for visually impaired users.

Temperature Conversion Interface & Fitt's Law Exercise

We were tasked with proposing a redesign of a presented temperature conversion interface. The goal was to make an interface whose average time to completion (TF), as calculated with Fitt's Law, was as low as possible.
